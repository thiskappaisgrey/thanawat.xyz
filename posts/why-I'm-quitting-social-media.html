The “benefits” of Social Media

The “benefits” of Social Media

  • Social Pressures and rewards
  • Benefits of Social Media
    • I get “friends” to like my posts
    • I get “friends” to see my stories
  • The reality of it is, nobody actually cares about me or what I do!
    • People have their own lives
    • I’m just another “face on the wall/stories”

Why are proprietary Social Media platforms bad?

Why are proprietary Social Media platforms bad?

  • They feed in to your own ideology
    • They can and do manipulate your feed, and indirectly manipulate your beliefs about the world
    • “Filter bubble” - you get information that confirms your beliefs
    • What applies to others also applies to you!
  • Violates Freedom of Speech
    • Censorship
  • Privacy - 1984
    • These companies don’t respect the user!
    • Knowledge is power. Giving Facebook my data is the same is giving Facebook the power over me.
  • The main reason I’m quitting is because it wastes my time
    • I can focus on creating more thoughtful and better information if I’m not so focused on checking Instagram for reactions to my posts
  • There are other platforms out there that are respectful of me, the user

Cost/Benefit analysis

Cost/Benefit analysis

  • Looking at both the implicit(opportunity and long term) costs and the supposed benefits of social media, the costs outweighs the benefits!
  • Human beings evolved to focus on the immediate benefit of things
    • This means that it’s harder for us to consider the long term costs
  • Social media is like cigarette smoking or gambling
    • It has immediate benefits but it’s bad for you in the long term
