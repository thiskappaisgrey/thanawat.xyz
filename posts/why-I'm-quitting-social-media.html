Quote

Quote

“Arguing that you don’t care about the right to privacy because you have nothing to hide, is no different than saying you don’t care about free speech because you have nothing to say.” - Edward Snowden

Why are proprietary Social Media platforms bad?

Why are proprietary Social Media platforms bad?

  • They are a centralized platform for discussion
    • This is bad b/c they can (and does) control your speech
  • Privacy - They have lots of information on you!
    • They probably know you more than you do
    • 1984
  • It wastes your time
    • Opportunity costs

The “benefits” of Social Media

The “benefits” of Social Media

  • Benefits of Social Media
    • I get “friends” to like my posts
    • I get “friends” to see my stories
  • The reality of it is, nobody actually cares about me or what I do!
    • People have their own lives
    • I’m just another “face on the wall/stories”

Cost/Benefit analysis

Cost/Benefit analysis

  • Human beings evolved to focus on the immediate benefit of things
    • This means that it’s harder for us to consider the long term costs
  • Social media is like cigarette smoking or gambling
    • It has immediate benefits but it’s bad for you in the long term
  • If you think Congress is going to do something about it, well I would politely call you a “fool”.
  • Looking at both the implicit(opportunity and long term) costs and the supposed benefits of social media, I decided that I should and will quit!

Alternative Proprietary Social Media
