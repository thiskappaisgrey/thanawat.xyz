Getting one year older..

Posted on August 22, 2020

Not getting wiser

I’m 19 now, and that’s pretty young. That means that if, big if here, I live to 80, I’ll have another 60 years left on this Earth. That’s pretty darn long? Maybe its not considering the Earth is billions of years old. Been thinking about dying and death recently. Maybe that’s just a part of being a living being. Death is inevitable yet we fear it nonetheless. Honestly, in the grand scheme of things, my life probably doesn’t really matter. Anyways, that doesn’t really matter now, because I’m alive and I don’t think I’ll die any time soon? Maybe I’ll get to live long enough to post this on my blog. If you’re reading this, then I’m probably alive? Well, anyways, I’ve been on this Earth for 19 years and I don’t think I’ve gotten that much wiser. Sure, I’m less dumb, but I’m still pretty dumb if you ask me. Maybe I did get wiser, and I’m just too dumb to notice? Yeah, that’s the writing style I’m going for in these posts! The self-cynical, train of thought, pointless rambling style that embodies my personality for the past couple of years. Maybe one day I’ll get wise enough to get over it but for now, I’m just a selfish, sad, lonely, depressed soul, scared of its own inevitable death, yet doesn’t really care anymore about anything. Not sure why that matters though. Life is kinda sad to be honest. Not much to really live for except maybe dying is kinda scary and dying will make my family sad and stuff. They did put all this effort into raising me after all, and they’re not even asking much of me, they just want me to be able to take care of myself. Hopefully I get wise enough by then, but right now I don’t have hope. Whenever I hope for something I always get disappointed, so better not have my hopes up and just live it out for a while, fully expecting the worst. That’s my 19 years of wisdom right there.

The Future

Maybe in a couple of years I’ll “get over” this sad phase, maybe get a job(or find a way to get money somehow), be independent, living on my own, all alone, and be happy? Doubt that’s gonna happen though. At least not the “happy” part. Like I said, hope is kinda pointless, because most likely I’ll just get disappointed. I’ll definitely try my best to get some sort of income for sure, I don’t want to depend on my parents forever! Maybe I’ll send my family some money, but I doubt it. Probably will be barely able to afford keeping myself alive after I finish college. I honestly don’t know what to do really. I don’t want to do “evil things”, and that pretty much crosses out a bunch of “great” companies. Maybe morality is all B.S. and I should just choose to do whatever will bring me the most benefit? But then again doing that’ll just make my life feel sad, and I don’t want to feel sad anymore than I already do. My dream scenario would be to work somewhere with code, be able to use Emacs everyday, work with Free(https://www.fsf.org/) and Open Source software, be able to afford coffee, and still have time to play Guitar and sing on the streets to random people to enjoy(or not? =P). The thing is though, dreams change as you grow older. Maybe you become disappointed that your dream will never be a reality or the illusion of money and power gets to you. I’ll probably become disappointed one day, because my dream will inevitably mismatch with reality. I’ll just try my best anyways, fully expecting to fail, but if I fail I’ll just stubbornly try again. Maybe I’ll fail and keep failing, who knows? Nothing really matters to me anyways, it’s not like my family is dependent on my success or anything! Anyways, as I’ve said in my high school senior quote, “Failure is an Option”.

What I’m doing now

After all that depressing talk, let’s be positive for just a teeny bit! I’m obviously working on this very website, thanawat.xyz! Maybe I’ll redesign it after a little bit, I’m not too sure. Also learning the Linux operating system, and currently learning the wonderful, purely functional, NixOS distribution of Linux. Been enjoying it so far, and probably will be using it for years to come(or maybe I’ll distrohop again in 6 months, who knows?). Also looking into self-hosting a lot of my stuff(potentially want to self-host an email server for fun) so that I don’t send off my data to evil companies. Self-hosting also provides great learning experience so that maybe I could get a system-administration job as a backup plan.

Another thing I’m doing is learning Emacs(as I’ve been doing for the past 2 years). I’m exploring Org Mode workflows, and how I could get more organized with my life. My goal is to reduce cognitive load to as little as possible so I can either: a. focus on doing something with my life and b. cry myself to bed because life is so depressing. Currently (re)reading Atomic Habits(I actually physically own this) a book about creating good habits and Getting Things Done, a rather popular book in the Org-Mode(and self-help) community. I probably won’t be reading any more self-help books as there’s probably no point after reading a couple of them and actually helping yourself do productive stuff.

One the of “productive” stuff I want to do is reading. My reading goals for this year(and the next couple of years) are reading more Nietzsche(Thus Spoke Zarathustra) , the Tipitaka(the Buddhist Scriptures), Marx(Das Kapital), Adam Smith(The Wealth of Nations ), Milton Freedman(Capitalism and Freedom ), Sigmund Freud(read some of his main works), Carl Jung(also some of his main works), and maybe I’ll get to like, the Bible or something way later down the line. Obviously this is a rather hopeful list, and I’m not a very hopeful person! However, I do have another(if I’m lucky) 60 years left to live, so maybe I will get to all of these works and more if I really tried.

Of course, since one aspect of my dream is to be able to play guitar to random people on the streets, I have to actually practice guitar! I have some good song ideas and found some good resources on learning guitar and music production on Youtube/LBRY, so I’ll probably be practicing and producing more songs in the future. (Also, been using run-ons a lot, but I really can’t help it, English education didn’t really help improve my writing skills).

I’m also getting into Calisthenics and Coffee! The Bodyweight Fitness Community 🏸 on Reddit has been really helpful for getting into calisthenics. I like the idea of doing Bodyweight stuff because I don’t want to ever get a gym membership, and I just want to be able to get fit any where I go. You’ve might have already noticed, I’m pretty obsessed with free and freedom lately, so Calisthenics just fits right into that. Another thing I want to improve on is brewing coffee! Although I don’t really need the caffeine of coffee, I just enjoy drinking it because it has a lot of intricate and unique flavors. Anyways, I’m not that good at either of these things yet, but I’m sure I’ll get good enough one day! Maybe I’ll do a post about them on this blog, not sure though.

Other than that I have some games in my Steam Library that I want to play, including: Dead Cells, Stardew Valley, Hollow Knight, Rocket League and much more! I’m more passionate about other things, but its good to take a break every once and a while.

Why this post sucks

I didn’t edit this post at all, like may pieces of my writing. The more I write, the better I’ll get, but I don’t really care about getting better or worse, I just want to write and put my thoughts out there unfiltered. Maybe some employer will see this(I doubt it.. I’ll just delete all my posts before then probably, or direct them to a portfolio site). I don’t want to filter my thoughts even more when I already filtered them! You don’t have to read all my posts, just the ones you’re interested in. Maybe you’re interested in none of them, and that’s OK! I’ll just be who I am, all the time, and if that bites me in the back someday then whatever, I don’t care. Or maybe I’ll be regretting really bad when I end up homeless, I don’t know.

Conclusion

Anyways, thanks for reading and I hope I didn’t waste your time. Or maybe I did, I don’t know. Have an awesome day!
