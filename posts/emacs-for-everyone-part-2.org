*How to Configure Doom Emacs*
There are 3 files that you edit in order to configure Doom:
1. =~/.doom.d/init.el=
   + You enable your modules here.
2. =~/.doom.d/config.el=
   + Extra configuration goes here.
3. =~/.doom.d/packages.el=
   + Extra packages(not bundled in modules) goes here.
* Slide 3
*The modules you need for a writing setup*
1. =org=
   + The most important piece you need for a writing setup
   + This mode/module does so much, and it's amazing!
   + *Optional* =+roam= Zettlekansten in Emacs
2. *Optional* =evil=
   + Vim emulation in Emacs, enabling this also enables other functionality not available in Vanilla Vim
3. *Optional* =zen=
   + Distraction Free writing
4. *Optional* =magit=
   + For collaboration
* Slide 4
*The writing workflow*
There are 3 main parts in writing:
1. Inspiration & Planning
2. Research
3. Synthesis

=org-mode= provides a set of tools that will assist you in completing these tasks!
* Slide 5
*Part 1: Planning*
When you want to write something, you have to first figure
out what you're going to write and how you are going to do it.
You don't have to use =Org-mode= for this, but you can!

Using =org-capture= and keeping a todo list in org allows you to
easily become more organized!
* Slide 6
*Part 2: Research*
Organize your thoughts in an =Org= file, or even better,
use =org-roam=!
* Slide 7
*Part 3: Synthesis*
Organize your writing and citations with =org-mode=, and let Emacs and
Latex automate everything else! No more worrying about if your citations
are correct or how your paper will look!
